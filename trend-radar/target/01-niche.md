# 01 · Target Niche

## Language / country
The language of your content and your audience's country. Example: "English · US". (Reddit is English-heavy; the system extracts the pattern and writes sample titles in your language.)

## Niche (one sentence)
What topic you make content on, for whom.
Example: "indoor plant care, practical tips for beginners."

## Subreddits (each one is scanned · at least 3, at most 6)
Write the communities CLOSEST to your niche. To find them: search your topic on reddit.com, or google "your topic + subreddit". Write the community name without `r/`, just the name.
- <subreddit 1>
- <subreddit 2>
- <subreddit 3>

> Tip: a narrow, niche-specific community gives a cleaner signal; a very broad community (millions of members, every topic) brings noise. Mix them: 1-2 broad + 2-3 narrow.

## Search terms (optional · for pasted examples)
If you are going to hand-paste standouts from YouTube/TikTok/Reels, which words you will search with. Words your audience actually uses.
- <term 1>
- <term 2>

## What you are not looking for (optional)
Topics/communities that look like your niche but are not your trend. Example: "outdoor garden communities, plant-selling groups."
