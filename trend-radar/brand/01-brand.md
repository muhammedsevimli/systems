In one sentence: who you are, what topic you make content on.

## For whom (your audience)
Who watches your content. Level (beginner / intermediate / advanced), interest, pain.

## Which platform + format

- Main format: <30-45 sec reel / 8-12 min video / carousel / thread>.

## What you make (your topic lane)
The lane your content stays in. Which sub-topics are your work.
- <sub-topic 1>
- <sub-topic 2>

## What you are NOT (off-lane · the system does not suggest these)
Topics that look like your niche but are NOT your work. If a trend falls into these, the system says "skip".
Example: "not sales/store content. not academic/technical depth. not news/current-affairs."

## Your voice anchor (optional)
The angle that sets you apart, your recurring lens. Example: "I show everything the cheap/practical way."
