## Who / what you make
I am a content creator making short, practical content on indoor houseplant care. I teach beginners "how to grow your plant without killing it".

## For whom (your audience)
People who just bought their first plant or killed a few, who do not know botany and want some greenery at home. Level: beginner.

## Which platform + format
- Main platform: Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts.
- Main format: 30-45 sec vertical video, karaoke captions.
- Cadence: 3 reels a week.

## What you make (your topic lane)
- indoor houseplant care (watering, light, pots, soil).
- rescuing / diagnosing a dying plant.
- cheap and practical fixes (propagation, saving a supermarket plant).
- beginner mistakes.

## What you are NOT (off-lane · the system does not suggest these)
- not outdoor garden / vegetable garden content (balcony pots are fine, field/garden no).
- not plant sales, store tours, collection flexing.
- not academic botany / latin species lessons (my audience is scared off).
- not clinical pest diagnosis (expert work); general prevention is fine, medical diagnosis no.

## Your voice anchor (optional)
I show everything the cheap and practical way. I do not sell pricey tools or special fertilizer; I solve it with what is in the kitchen.
