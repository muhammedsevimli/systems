## Address
- Instagram / TikTok / X: informal "you".
- LinkedIn / email: measured, professional register.
- Do not mix the two in the same piece.

## Words you use
Expressions that are yours and come up often. Titles are built with these.

## BANNED words / patterns (the system never uses them)
- em dash.
- clickbait lies: "the secret nobody knows", "99% do this wrong" (unproven).
- forced contrast: "it is not X, it is Y" / "the real issue is".
- motivational language, guru talk.
- hype adjectives: "amazing, incredible, revolutionary".
- fake urgency: "do not miss", "last chance".
- <words you dislike>.
