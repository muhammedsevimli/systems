# 02 · Voice

> How you write. The system produces every concept to this tone and this banned list.
> The most critical file: this is where the system actually sounds like you, so the competitor's ad does not come out of your mouth.
> If you already set up a brand memory or carousel generator, copy the same one.

## Tone (3-5 adjectives)
Example: "clear, warm, understated, reassuring, sincere."

## How you address
- Instagram / Facebook ad: "you" (informal, direct).
- LinkedIn: "you" (more measured, professional).
Do not mix the two registers inside the same concept. The system switches to the measured register automatically for the LinkedIn version.

## Words you use
Expressions of yours that show up often. Concepts become your voice with these.
Example: "daily, home, comfort, cotton, plain."

## BANNED words / patterns (the system NEVER uses)
- em dash (long dash).
- "it is not X it is Y" / "the real point is" style fake contrast.
- motivational language, "change your life" style.
- fake urgency: "last 24 hours", "stocks running out", exclamation storms (even if the competitor uses them).
- empty adjectives: "amazing, incredible, awesome, legendary".
- income or number flexing.
