# 10 Concepts · Output Format

> After the analysis, the system writes exactly 10 ad concepts. All of them follow this structure.
> The goal: not to start from a blank page. To put your product on top of a logic that clearly works.

## Structure of the output file
The system writes the output to `outputs/YYYY-MM-DD-<theme>.md` in this order:

### A) Competitor ad breakdown table
Each analyzed ad is one row: competitor · hook type · promise/offer · for whom · format · how long it has been running · winning candidate or not.

### B) Extracted pattern (one paragraph)
The repeating hook, the common offer type, the common emotion, who they speak to. "The logic that clearly works" is summarized here in one paragraph.

### C) 10 concepts
Each concept has these six lines:

```text
### Concept N · <short name>
- hook: <opening line in your brand's voice>
- format: <video / single image / carousel> suggestion + one-line scene/visual idea
- promise/offer: <your real offer; otherwise [put your own offer here]>
- cta: <what you will ask: come to the site / dm / see the offer>
- which competitor logic it came from: <which part of the pattern>
- why it can land: <one-line reason>
```

### D) Platform note
Ready in the "you" form for Instagram/Facebook. At the end, a LinkedIn measured-register note.

## Distribution of the 10 concepts (variety rule)
The system does not squeeze all 10 concepts into one hook. It spreads the different angles that come out of the pattern:
- 3-4 concepts from the strongest pattern (your version of the hook that has run for months).
- 2-3 concepts from the second pattern (a different offer type or a different segment).
- 2-3 concepts from a counter angle / gap: the angle the competitor does NOT use but which fits your differentiation (from the "why you" line in `01-brand.md`).
This way you use both the proven logic and the gap the competitor cannot see.

## Hard rules
- Do not copy the competitor's sentence. Take its logic, not its words.
- No made-up numbers / discounts / guarantees. If it is not in `01-brand.md`, use a placeholder.
- Tone from `02-voice.md`. No banned words. No em dash.
- Each concept carries one idea, the hook is a single clear sentence.
