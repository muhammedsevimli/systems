# 01 · Core

> The unchanging foundation. Who you are, what you do, for whom, what you are after right now.
> Read on every task. Rarely changes, review once a month.

## Who / what we do
What [the person or business] does in one sentence.
Example: "A one-person studio that builds websites and digital order for small businesses."

## For whom
Who you serve. Age, sector, pain.
Example: "Tradespeople and small service businesses with no website or an outdated one."

## Current priority (this quarter)
What you are focused on most. The system prioritizes work accordingly.
Example: "Growing sales of the monthly recurring maintenance package. We are not looking for one-off projects."

## What we are NOT
The boundary. When the system reads these lines it does not head the wrong way.
Example: "We are not a big corporate agency. We are not a place that mass-produces cheap sites."

## Proof / trust
Numbers, references, track record. Credibility comes from these.
Example: "Since 2021 · 40+ businesses · delivered in 3 weeks on average."
