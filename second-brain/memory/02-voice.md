# 02 · Voice

> How you write. The system follows the tone in this file when producing text.
> Update it when the tone of your output starts to drift.

## Tone (3-5 adjectives)
Example: "clear, warm, understated, reassuring."

## Address
How you speak to people, and where.
Example: "'you' formal on the website and in email, 'you' casual on instagram."

## Words you use
Phrases that are yours, that come up often.
Example: "order, delivery, maintenance, a site that works."

## BANNED words / patterns
The system must NEVER use these.
Example: "'last chance', 'don't miss out', exclamation-mark spam, too many emojis, empty adjectives (amazing, awesome)."
