# 04 · People (customers, partners, relationships)

> Who you work with, who matters for what, what was last discussed.
> The answer to "where are we with this customer" lives here.

## How to use
- For each person/business: name, relationship, what they matter for, latest status.
- Add someone new when they come in. Move a relationship to the archive when it ends.
- Do NOT write private info (phone, contract figures) here. Only note "where it is kept".
