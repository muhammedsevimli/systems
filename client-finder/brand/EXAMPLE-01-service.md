## Who you are / what you do
I produce Meta ad creative (ad copy + visual direction) for small e-commerce brands and put their ads into a test loop.

## For whom (ideal client)
E-commerce brands doing between $10k and $100k a month, running ads on Instagram/Facebook but with no in-house ad team. Usually the founder writes the ads themselves and does not have the time.

## What you sell (packages + real price range)
- Package 1: Creative Testing · 8 ad creatives a month + a weekly test report · $800-1,200/month
- Package 2: Full Creative Line · 20 creatives a month + visual direction + monthly strategy · $1,800-2,400/month
- Smallest unit of work: a one-off 5-creative trial package · $400

## Which problems you solve (weak point mapping)
- weak hook: YES, my main work. I build the first sentence of the ad from the customer's own problem.
- unclear offer: YES. I put a clear offer and a single button goal into the ad copy.
- single format: YES, my main work. I produce variants that test the same offer in 4 different formats.
- no social proof: PARTLY. I turn customer reviews into ad copy, but I do not set up review collection or surveys.

## Proof / why you
I ran the creative line of a home textiles brand for 4 months, and we moved from a single visual to a 4-format test. I work like someone who sets up tests, not like a copywriter: every creative is a hypothesis.

## What you are NOT
- I do not send bulk cold email.
- I do not manage ad accounts (budget/targeting stays with the client), I only produce creative.
- I do not promise guaranteed results (I will not say "I will triple it").
- I do not do websites, logos, or social media content calendars.
