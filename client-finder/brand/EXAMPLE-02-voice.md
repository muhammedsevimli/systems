# 02 · Voice (FILLED-IN EXAMPLE · fictional)

> This is an example. Write your own voice into `brand/02-voice.md`.

## Tone (3-5 adjectives)
Clear, calm, understated, curious, no sales pressure. I talk like someone who knows ads, but I do not talk down.

## Register
- Instagram / X DM: direct and casual, first name basis.
- LinkedIn / email: a more measured register.

## Words you use
"hook", "test", "variant", "first second", "scrolled past", "hypothesis". Plain English, no unnecessary jargon.

## BANNED words / patterns (the system never uses them)
- em dash (long dash).
- bulk-send language: "hello, I would like to introduce our services", "we would love to work with you", "our company, since ...".
- empty flattery: "you have built an amazing brand", "I love your content".
- artificial contrast in the "it is not X, it is Y" / "the real issue is" style.
- motivational language, guru language.
- fake urgency: "this week only", "last 3 spots".
- exaggerated adjectives: "amazing, incredible, revolutionary".
- flexing revenue or numbers.
- a guarantee I do not give: "I will triple it", "results guaranteed".
- ending with two questions, or a pile of options.

## Sample sentences (in your voice)
- "when the first sentence opens with the brand name, people usually scroll past in the second second."
- "the only way to know is to test the two variants side by side."
- "I have an idea here, and trying it is about a week of work."
