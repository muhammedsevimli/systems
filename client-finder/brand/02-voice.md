# 02 · Voice

> The messages go out in your words. This file describes how you write.

## Tone (3-5 adjectives)
E.g. "clear, calm, understated, curious, no sales pressure."

## Register
- Instagram / X DM: direct and casual, first name basis.
- LinkedIn / email: a more measured register, still not stiff.
- Do not mix the two in the same message.

## Words you use
Expressions that are yours, the ones that come up often.

## BANNED words / patterns (the system never uses them)
- em dash (long dash).
- bulk-send language: "hello, I would like to introduce our services", "we would love to work with you", "our company, since ...".
- empty flattery: "you have built an amazing brand", "I love your content".
- artificial contrast in the "it is not X, it is Y" / "the real issue is" style.
- motivational language, guru language.
- fake urgency: "this week only", "last 3 spots".
- exaggerated adjectives: "amazing, incredible, revolutionary".
- flexing revenue or numbers.
- a guarantee you do not give: "I will triple it", "results guaranteed".
- ending with two questions, or a pile of options ("A or B, or if you like C").
