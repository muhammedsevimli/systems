# 01 · Target Niche

> Which niche you are prospecting in. The system builds the Meta Ad Library search links from here.
> A filled-in example: `target/EXAMPLE-01-niche.md`.

## Country
A two-letter code. E.g. `US`. (Turkey `TR`, Germany `DE`, Netherlands `NL`.)

## Niche (one sentence)
Which industry, what type of business you are looking for. E.g. "boutique jewelry brands selling online in the United States."

## Search terms (each one becomes a separate link)
The words you will search in the library. Write the word the audience actually uses, not the corporate definition. Add synonyms too; one term means one list.
- <term 1>
- <term 2>
- <term 3>

## What you will not search for (optional)
Types that look like your niche but are not your client. E.g. "chain retailers, agencies (they do this themselves)."
