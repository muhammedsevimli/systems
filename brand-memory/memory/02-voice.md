# 02 · Voice

> How your brand talks. The system follows the tone in this file in every piece of text.
> Update it once a month, when the tone of your output starts to drift.

## Tone (3-5 adjectives)
How your brand sounds.
Example: "warm, honest, knowledgeable but not smug, calm."

## Address
How you speak to the audience: casual or formal. Where each one.
Example: "casual on Instagram, a bit more formal in email."

## Words you use
Your brand's natural vocabulary. Frequent phrases that are yours.
Example: "table, harvest, grower, palate, real."

## BANNED words / patterns
The system must NEVER use these. Language that breaks the tone and is not yours.
Example: "'don't miss out', 'last chance', exclamation-mark spam, too many emojis, 'legendary taste'."

## Sample sentences (in your voice)
2-3 sentences you actually wrote or would write. The system learns the tone from these.
- "..."
- "..."

## Emoji / punctuation preference
Example: "few emojis, not at the end of a sentence. no exclamation marks. ellipses are fine."
