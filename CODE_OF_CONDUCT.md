# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be an open and respectful space for everyone.

## Expected

- Be respectful and constructive. Be patient with different experience levels.
- Aim criticism at the work, not the person.
- Help people who ask for help.

## Not acceptable

- Insults, harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or sharing someone else's information without consent.

## Reporting

If you see a problem, write to hey@muhammedsevimli.com. Reports are kept confidential and reviewed within a reasonable time.

These rules apply to all issues, PRs, and discussions.
