# 02 · Voice

> How you write. The system produces every concept in this tone and against this banned list.
> The most critical file: the system resembles you mainly through here, so the concepts read as if they came from your mouth.
> If you already set up Brand Memory, Ad Spy, or the Carousel Generator, copy the same file.

## Tone (3-5 adjectives)
Example: "clear, warm, understated, trustworthy, personal."

## Address
- Instagram / Facebook ad: "you" (informal, direct).
- LinkedIn: a measured, professional register.
Do not mix them within the same concept. The system switches automatically for the LinkedIn version.

## Words you use
Expressions that are yours, that recur. The concepts become your voice with these.
Example: "fresh, roast date, cup, ritual, morning, aroma."

## BANNED words / patterns (the system NEVER uses)
- em dash (long dash).
- "it's not X, it's Y" / "the real point is" style artificial contrast.
- motivational language, "change your life" style.
- fake urgency: "last 24 hours", "stock is running out", exclamation storms (even if it appears in a review or an old ad).
- empty adjectives: "amazing, incredible, magical, legendary".
- income/number flexing.
- a guarantee you do not offer.
- (whatever else you want to add) ...
