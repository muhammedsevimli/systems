# EXAMPLE · how to fill the input (fictional)

> This file is just an example. It shows how the three inputs look, through a fictional brand "Değirmen Coffee".
> In your own work, put these three sections into separate files: `winning-ads.md`, `customer-reviews.md`, `liked-comments.md`.

## 1) Winning ads

## Ad 1
Hook (first sentence): do you know when your supermarket coffee was roasted?
Promise / offer: we roast when the order comes in, the roast date is printed on the pack.
Format: video
Performance note: best-performing ad for 8 months.
Text: do you know when your supermarket coffee was roasted? most of it, months ago. we roast when your order comes in, and the roast date is printed on the box. 20% off your first box.

---

## Ad 2
Hook (first sentence): the moment you turn the grinder in the morning, the smell of your home changes.
Promise / offer: fresh beans, grind at home.
Format: single image
Performance note: running for 6 months.
Text: the moment you turn the grinder in the morning, the smell of your home changes. that is what fresh beans are. fresh-roasted coffee at your door every month.

---

## Ad 3
Hook (first sentence): third-wave coffee at 4 units a cup.
Promise / offer: subscription value, cheaper than a cafe.
Format: carousel
Performance note: running for 5 months, the value angle.
Text: a coffee outside costs 90. at home, drink the same quality at 4 a cup. monthly subscription, cancel whenever you want.

---

## Ad 4
Hook (first sentence): what do you get someone who loves coffee?
Promise / offer: gift subscription.
Format: single image
Performance note: seasonal, good in the gifting period.
Text: what do you get someone who loves coffee? a subscription that sends fresh coffee to their door every month. we add your note to the box too.
