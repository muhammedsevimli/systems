# This is where you paste customer reviews

> This file is a guide. Do not paste reviews into this file; paste them into NEW files you open in this folder (e.g. `moisturizer.md`).
> The system does NOT include the guide file starting with `READ-` in the analysis; it only reads the review files you paste.

## Where and how you copy reviews (platform by platform)
- **Trendyol / Amazon / big marketplaces:** go to the "Reviews / Ratings" section on the product page, click "See all", and as you scroll the reviews load. Select with the mouse, copy, paste here. If you grab the rating and date too, the system works better.
- **Instagram / Facebook comments:** select the comments under the post by hand, copy. Grab the standout questions and praise.
- **Shopify / Judge.me / Loox widget:** select from the review box on the product page, copy. Open "See all" if it exists.
- **Google / Etsy / reviews on your own site:** same logic, select the visible reviews and copy.
- **WhatsApp / DM feedback:** you can also add the satisfaction or complaint messages customers wrote to you directly; these are gold.

## Paste template (for each review, keep it plain)
Pasting one under another like below is enough. Add the rating and date if present, leave blank if not.

```text
## Review 1
Rating: 5
Date: (if any)
Review: paste the customer's text here.

## Review 2
Rating: 2
Review: ...
```

Actually the fastest: without worrying about ratings and numbering, paste the reviews one under another and separate each with `---`. The system reads both.

## Tips
- **Grab both high and low ratings.** Sales angles come from the five stars, objection responses come from the one and two stars. If you only take the nice reviews, you will not see the most common objection.
- At least 8-10 reviews, ideally 20-50. The more reviews, the clearer the themes.
- You can put all reviews of the same product in one file, and different products in separate files.
- Do not try to fix or shorten the reviews; the raw form is best. The customer's own words are the raw material of the sales copy.
