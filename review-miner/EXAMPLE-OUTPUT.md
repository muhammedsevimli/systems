## D) Sales arsenal

### Theme 1 · Jar is small (the most common objection)
- **sales angle:** the jar is not small, you use little. a hazelnut-sized amount covers the whole face, one jar lasts for months.
- **objection response:** if you're thinking "the jar is tiny, it'll run out fast", a customer's words are exactly for you: "i use a tiny bit and it covers my whole face, one jar hasn't run out in two months." we currently make one size, and we do not add filler to make it look full; because a little is enough, it goes a long way.
- **ad hook:** if "the jar is small" put you off, listen to the math of someone who uses it: a hazelnut-sized amount covers the whole face, one jar does not run out for months.
- **which reviews it came from:** R3 "smaller than i expected", R19 "really tiny, please make a big size", R16 "runs out fast", R20 "the size is small"; refuted by R6 "one jar hasn't run out in two months".

### Theme 2 · Expensive / price
- **sales angle:** look not at the per-jar price but at the daily cost. because a little is used and it lasts for months, the daily care cost stays low.
- **objection response:** to "expensive", the same customer has the answer: "when you do the math it's even cheap." we do not put paraben, mineral oil, or synthetic fragrance inside; we produce small-batch. the price means clean content and long life from little use, not cheap filler.
- **ad hook:** if one jar of a natural, additive-free cream lasts two months, do you think that's expensive?
- **which reviews it came from:** R5 "a bit expensive", R14 "felt expensive"; refuted by R6 "when you do the math it's even cheap". Note: since the brand is not a discount/campaign brand (R14 "if there were a campaign i'd buy in bulk"), no discount angle was built.

### Theme 3 · Winter skin dryness and flaking (the strongest reason for buying)
- **sales angle:** daily care for dry skin whose cheeks flake in winter and whose makeup won't hold. the first step to rescue your skin from dryness.
- **objection response:** if you wonder "how many days to take effect": it varies by skin, we do not give a clear day promise. one customer said "my skin got moisturized in a week", another "the dryness was gone in two weeks"; the experience differs from person to person.
- **ad hook:** if your cheeks flake in winter, a hazelnut-sized amount at night is enough.
- **which reviews it came from:** R1 "the dryness is gone", R9 "my skin was very dry, smooth when applied underneath", R17 "the flaking went away", R18 "it's become my winter care"; for timeframe R8 "in a week", R1 "in two weeks".

### Theme 4 · Sensitive skin, does not sting
- **sales angle:** for sensitive skin that reddened with other creams, that stung even with a pharmacy cream, this gives no reaction.
- **objection response:** if you're wary of whether your sensitive skin will take it: two customers had years of stinging with branded and pharmacy creams, and wrote that they got no reaction from this. you also have a 14-day return, try it with ease.
- **ad hook:** if branded creams redden your face, there is a light and comfortable option for sensitive skin.
- **which reviews it came from:** R4 "branded creams made my face red, this gave no reaction at all", R15 "even pharmacy creams stung, this didn't sting at all".

### Theme 5 · Additive-free content, avoiding chemicals
- **sales angle:** no paraben, mineral oil, or synthetic fragrance. clean content for those who want to know what they put on their skin.
- **objection response:** if you want to trust the content: we write openly both what we put in and what we do not. as one customer said, "the ingredients are spotless, no paraben or mineral oil."
- **ad hook:** if you don't want to put chemicals on your skin, we write openly what we do NOT put in too.
- **which reviews it came from:** R7 "the ingredients are spotless, no paraben or mineral oil", R12 "being natural gives trust, we don't want to put chemicals on".

### Theme 6 · Not greasy, fast absorption, under makeup
- **sales angle:** it absorbs the moment you apply it, does not leave a greasy feel. you can do your makeup underneath in the morning without waiting.
- **objection response:** if you're wary of "a cream leaves grease, makeup slides", two customers wrote that it absorbs the moment it's applied and stays smooth under makeup.
- **ad hook:** for skin in a morning rush: it absorbs the moment you apply it, you can do your makeup right away.
- **which reviews it came from:** R9 "smooth when applied underneath, and doesn't leave it greasy", R11 "it absorbs the moment you apply it, i can do my makeup right away".
