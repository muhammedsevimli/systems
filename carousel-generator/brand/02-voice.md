> How you write. The system produces every slide to this tone and this banned list.

## Tone (3-5 adjectives)
Example: "clear, warm, understated, reassuring, straight to the point."

## How you address
- Instagram carousel: "you" (informal, direct).
- LinkedIn carousel: "you" (more measured, professional).
Do not mix the two registers inside the same carousel. The system switches to the measured register automatically for the LinkedIn version.

## Words you use
Expressions of yours that show up often. Carousels become your voice with these.
Example: "order, plan, flow, a system that works."

## BANNED words / patterns (the system NEVER uses)
- em dash (long dash).
- "it is not X it is Y" / "the real point is" style fake contrast.
- motivational language, "achieve / unlock your potential" style.
- "last chance", "do not miss out", exclamation storms.
- empty adjectives: "amazing, incredible, awesome".
- income or number flexing.
