## Product / service
**Chairtime** · an online booking page plus automatic SMS and email reminders for small local businesses (barber, hair salon, dental clinic, vet).

In one sentence: the customer books online, the system sends an automatic reminder before the appointment, and the business loses fewer hours to people who do not turn up.

## Who you sell to
- Single location local businesses with 1 to 8 staff.
- The decision maker is usually the owner. Not a developer, runs everything from a phone.
- Secondary target: small chains with 2 to 5 locations.

## Your region and the currency you sell in
- Region / market: one country, English speaking market.
- The currency you price in: USD.
- Do you have customers in another region: no. Maybe later.

## How you price today
A single price, 29 dollars a month. How it was arrived at: "50 feels like too much, 20 looks cheap", by gut feel. No tiers, everyone gets the same thing.

## Your cost structure (the floor you cannot price below)
- **Fixed cost:** servers and infrastructure, about 60 dollars a month in total, independent of customer count.
- **Variable cost:** SMS. Every message sent is real money. That is why no tier can have "unlimited SMS".
- **Email:** negligible at the volume used.
- **Labour:** setup support is about 1 hour per customer, in the first month.

> Write your cost as a figure when you fill this in. The system reads the price floor from here. If you do not write it, it marks "no cost information given" and does no floor check.

## Capacity
Solo operator. The number of customers I can support at once is limited, so a very cheap and very crowded base is not carryable for me.

## What you do NOT do (the what you are not list · the system obeys this)
- I do not do accounting or invoicing.
- I do not do stock or till tracking.
- I am not a payment institution, collection runs through a third party payment provider.
- I do not do on site installation and I do not sell hardware.
- I do not run a call centre.

## Your red lines on the pricing decision
- I do not want to change the price three times a year, whatever structure I set should hold for at least 6 months.
- I do not want a free forever plan, I cannot carry the support load.
- I do not want to promise unlimited SMS.
