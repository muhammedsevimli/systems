## Who you are / what you do
I run Kerbside Digital, a one person studio. I build simple websites and small digital tools for local businesses on the high street.

## Who you are building for (the niche)
- Neighbourhood businesses: bakeries, salons, cafes, boutiques. Places with 1 to 5 staff.
- The owners are not technical. They check things on a phone, standing behind a counter, and they do not read long dashboards.
- Businesses that can pay 20 to 100 dollars a month for a tool, not more.

## Your region and how you collect payment
- Region / market: one mid sized city, English speaking market.
- How you collect payment today: I invoice and take bank transfer, yearly up front. I do not run card subscriptions.
- What payment habit your customers have: they dislike monthly card subscriptions and forget to cancel things. They would rather pay once a year and be done.

## This audience's reality
- My customers' sites get 500 to 5000 visitors a month. Not much.
- My customer does not ask "how many people visited". They ask "how many people called me".
- They do not read a report. They want one screenshot in a message.
- Their site is four pages: home, menu or services, about, contact.

## What you can build (your own build power)
- Coding level: I do not write code. I build with Claude Code.
- Comfortable with: simple web panel, forms, tables, scheduled jobs, automatic email, generating an image file.
- Struggle with or avoid: mobile apps, complex payment infrastructure, real time big data.
- Hours per week: 8 to 10.
- Build budget: low per month. Free or cheap tiers first.

## What you can sell (your collection reality)
- Payment: bank transfer and invoice for now, payment infrastructure later.
- Reach: 20 small business customers already on my books.

## Your limits
- I do not build anything that stores personal data or matches identities.
- I do not build a one to one copy product. If I am not differentiating, I am not going in.
- I do not want work that needs live support around the clock.
- I do not go into regulated fields like health, finance or legal.

## Why you (your unfair advantage)
- 20 businesses are already my customers. I do not have to go find the first users, I already have their phone numbers.
- I know exactly what these owners ask, because I have been answering it for three years.
- I built their sites, so I can install anything myself without the owner touching a line of code.
