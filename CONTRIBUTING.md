# Contributing

This repo is community-supported and shared as-is. The systems are kept simple so anyone can adapt them to their own brand.

## Found a bug or have an idea?

- **Open an issue:** If something does not work or a system could be improved, open an issue on GitHub. Briefly say what you expected and what happened.
- **Send a PR:** For small fixes (typos, wording, examples) you can open a pull request directly. For bigger changes, open an issue first so we can talk it through.

## Scope

The systems here are templates you fill with your own brand. Do not send the files you filled with your own brand (like the contents of `memory/`) as a PR; those are yours and stay in your own copy.
